About the Application

This application is a data-driven underwriting analytics platform built using historical data from crop insurance schemes in India, including PMFBY and WBCIS.

It is designed to support insurers, policymakers, and analysts in evaluating the performance and risk characteristics of agricultural insurance portfolios across states and districts.

Key Objectives
Enable granular assessment of insurance performance across geographies
Support data-driven underwriting and pricing decisions
Identify profitable, stable, and high-risk regions
Facilitate portfolio design and diversification strategies
Core Features
Integration of multi-year data on:
Premiums
Claims
Sum insured
Computation of key underwriting metrics:
Loss ratio (burn rate)
Premium rate
Inter-annual variability
Interactive filtering by:
Year
Season (Kharif / Rabi)
Scheme (PMFBY / WBCIS)
Multi-level analysis:
State-level performance trends
District-level drilldowns
Custom portfolio builder:
Create clusters of districts
Simulate combined performance
Evaluate risk-return characteristics
Use Cases
Underwriting strategy and pricing decisions
Identification of stable vs volatile geographies
Portfolio diversification and risk balancing
Performance benchmarking across schemes and seasons
Data Source

Data used in this application is derived from publicly available records of:

Government crop insurance schemes (PMFBY, WBCIS)
